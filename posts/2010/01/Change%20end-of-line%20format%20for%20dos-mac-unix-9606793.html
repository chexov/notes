Change end-of-line format for dos-mac-unix

January 7 2010, 5:17 PM  by Anton Checkhov

To process, say, all *.txt files in the current directory:

vim *.txt
:set hidden
:bufdo %s/\r$//e
:bufdo %s/\r/ /eg
:xa
