Bacula. How to remove purged volumes from the disk.

July 2 2010, 3:12 PM  by Anton Checkhov
List of volumes which is not in use:

sqlite3 /var/spool/bacula/bacula.db "select VolumeName from Media where VolStatus != 'Used'"


How to remove bacula Volumes which are not in use:

sqlite3 /var/spool/bacula/bacula.db "select VolumeName from Media where VolStatus != 'Used'" | xargs -INAME rm -v /ebs/bacula/storage/NAME
